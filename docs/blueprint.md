# **App Name**: Falak Enterprises

## Core Features:

- Hero Section with Parallax and Animation: Full-screen hero section with a parallax background, fade-up, and slide-in text animations for the tagline and buttons linking to 'Our Services' and 'Contact Us'. Placeholder for hero image.
- Introduction Section: Two-column layout to display content from the 'INTRODUCTION' section of the PDF. Reveal animations on content load, enhanced with icons representing Experience, Creativity, and Quality.
- Our Services Section: Animated cards showcasing services: Interior Painting, Exterior Painting, Wood Polish Painting, Texture Painting, Waterproofing Services, and POP Installations. Each card includes a hover lift animation, relevant icon, and image placeholder.
- Why Choose Us Section: Displays text from the PDF, emphasizing experience, skilled workforce, and customer-centric approach. Each point is highlighted with an icon and a subtle fade-in text animation.
- Gallery Section: A dynamic masonry or grid layout gallery for showcasing completed projects, with zoom animations on image hover. Image placeholders are provided.
- Contact Section with Form and Map: Contact information including address and phone number from the PDF. Includes a contact form, a Google Maps placeholder, and animated icons for contact details.
- Elegant Footer: Simple footer containing the company name and contact details for easy access.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Royal Blue (#0F1E47) for a professional and premium feel.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F6F6F6), providing a subtle contrast that doesn't distract.
- Accent color: Gold (#D4AF37) for highlights and calls to action to create a sense of luxury.
- Heading font: 'Poppins', a bold, modern sans-serif, providing a premium look.
- Body font: 'Inter', a clean and readable sans-serif, ensuring legibility.
- Use high-quality, modern icons that complement the content. Icons should be animated subtly on hover to provide interactivity.
- Mobile-first responsive design with clean spacing and strategic use of glassmorphism and subtle shadows to enhance depth and visual appeal.
- Smooth scroll animations, parallax effects, and image reveal animations to create an engaging and premium user experience.
